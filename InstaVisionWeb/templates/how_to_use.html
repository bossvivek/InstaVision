How to Use the Instagram Following and Follower Comparison Tool.

  1. Open Instagram on your computer, browse to your profile, and click Edit Profile in the profile section.
  2. In the settings section, click on Meta account center.
  3. Click on the highlighted "Your information and permissions" section in the left sidebar.
  4. Click on the "Download your information" section.
  5. Click on the "Download or transfer information" button.
  6. Select "Some of your information" as highlighted.
  7. Scroll down to the connections section and select "Following and followers".
  8. Click on the "Download to device" button.
  9. Select your preferred date range and make sure the rest of the options look as shown in the image below. Then, click Create files.
  10. Wait for the files to be created. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of your data.
  11. Once the files are ready, you will receive a notification in your email. Click the download files link in the email to download the files.
  12. After downloading, you will have two files: followers.html and following.html. Upload them in the appropriate sections.
  13. Click the Compare button to see the comparison results.
  14. ENJOY! You can now see the common and unique followers and following between the two files.
